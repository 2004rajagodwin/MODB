What is Building Energy Modeling?

BEM uses advanced software tools to simulate a building’s energy consumption, thermal comfort, lighting, and HVAC performance. It enables data-driven decisions throughout the design and operational phases of a building’s lifecycle.

Energy Model Report Output

Our Energy Model Report presents detailed insights through visual data and simulations, similar to advanced software reports like IES VE. It includes both analytical and graphical outputs for quick understanding and decision-making.

  • Project Summary
  • 3D Building Simulation View
  • Monthly Energy Dashboard
  • Energy Flow Diagram (Sankey)
  • Annual Energy Consumption
  • Energy Use Intensity (EUI)
  • Annual Fuel Cost & Peak Demand
  • Peak Load Profiles
